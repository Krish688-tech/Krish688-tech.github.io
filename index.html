Know What is phishing and how to avoid phishing attacks.

How to avoid phishing attacks ?

Social Engineering attack is a widely-spread threat on the Internet and consists of an attacker designed to lure victims into entering sensitive information like passwords or credit card numbers by sending a fake website links via SMS, eMail, phone calls, Social Media Networks, Pdf Documents, videos links, Music links, software links, Android Applications links, Microsoft Documents links, Google Drive links, fake Amazon and Flipkart Offer links etc.,

The increased use of mobile applications in both business and private contexts makes them an increasingly popular channel for social engineering attacks.

More and more peoples use their smartphones to check their company e-mails or to read documents that are stored in the cloud. However, many smartphone users use highly vulnerable smartphone applications that can be misused to conduct social engineering attacks.

Moreover, some smartphone applications request permissions to access sensitive data on the user’s device. If an attacker were to create such an fake application, Once you install this application they would obtain the information and could use it as a starting point for a social engineering attack and Game over!

Don't panic and afraid, Now we are discussed about How to avoid and Keep safe your Accounts from these Social Engineering attacks like Phishing

1.What is Phishing

Phishing is one of the social engineering attack and the most talked about topic in the world of social engineering attack is phishing.

Phishing Starts with a fraudulent email that messages is made to looks as though it comes from a trusted sender, When you click that link they steal sensitive informations like your Credit card details and paypal accounts details, social media passwords, or to install malware on the victim's machine.

95% of Phishing attacks arrive by email 4% are carried out through malicious websites and 1% via fake phone calls called Vishing and fake text Message called SMSishing and don’t careless about that 1%.

How do Protect against Phishing attacks?

1.Know how that a phishing scam looks like and how to avoid?

Generally that look like with unrecognized greetings, the content of message doesn't have proper Grammar, Punctuation, Spellings, and the message content creates a sense of curiosity hackers uses psychological tactics to manipulate people. This kind of manipulation includes creating a sense of urgency or instilling fear, Simply Don’t panic and report this mail is spam and delete it.

2.Don't click on that link

Generally not advisable to click on a link in email or text messages just hovering over the link to see if the destination URL is the correct one, if you’re not recognize that link is trusted one ignore it, remember generally banks and popular websites did not ask any personal credentials via mail or text messages or phone calls.

3.Don't click any pop-up Windows

Generally unsecured website have most vulnerable popups, please do not click that popups.

In Some websites asks Login with Facebook account or any other social media account is a safe method and is being used by large number of website to make it easier for website visitors to sign-up for a third-party service.

When you click that login link of facebook.com from that website it redirects the link and pop up one facebook login window.

Now we listen carefully First thing the main source website is genuine or secured website it links starts with https, that doesn't start with https don’t fill any personal credentials in the pop up window.

The real popup cannot be moved when you drag or moved around, but fake popup can move around and For the smartphones the keyboard will appears automatically with real authentication popup window, but the fake popup requires you to click inside of the fields before the keyboard can be accessed.

Because its look like original URL to the facebook.com login page with green lock pad with valid HTTPS. Hackers created this fake pop up login with HTML and JavaScript, are perfectly Clone the exact facebook login page that gives feel exactly like a original facebook.com login page. Simply click “X” Close button in one of the corners of that popups.

So be Awareness of pop-up windows, But Facebook and other social media networks finds this loop holes give proper security update for this kind of attack so don’t ignore any updates.

5.Don't give your information to an unsecured site

Generally URL of the website doesn't start with HTTPS or you cannot see a closed padlock icon next to the URL, do not enter any sensitive information and cannot download any files from that site, You 100% trust the site you are on Make sure, you have to provide information, that verify the website is genuine, that the company is real and that website is secure itself.

6.Maintenance of Your accounts

Keep safe and properly maintain your Apple mail, Microsoft outlook and google mail accounts always use a strong password, change your passwords regularly and avoid reusing old passwords, add extra security and make sure two step verification is on keep updates on your accounts.

If you are login your email accounts in any website don’t forget to logout, use only trusted, secured and authorized websites and applications.

7.Regularly Change passwords

If you are often more money transactions or regularly purchase products form online shopping, The habit of regularly Changing your passwords so that prevent you from attacker gaining access that gives extra layer of protection through password changing can prevent ongoing attacks.

8.Don't ignore any important updates

Most number of peoples turn off or ignore the update message because of receiving numerous update messages this makes frustrating so please don’t do this.

Most common reason for updates are to keep up to date with modern cyber-attack methods by patching holes in security, If you are do not update your computers, Smartphones or browsers or important security applications, you could be at risk of phishing attacks.

Firewalls gives an effective way to prevent attacks, acting as a barrier shield between your computer and hackers, so properly update firewall security patch and do not turn off Firewalls during some software installation in online or offline do not use firewall’s blocked software applications.
